AI Pre-Call Brief

ClaudeDashboard
Before the call Claude surfaces a brief in the dashboard: LinkedIn profile, Google search, Zoho history, prior emails, contractor size and trade type. Brock walks into every call prepared. ⚠ HOOK/CLAUDE: web search + Zoho MCP + Gmail scan — buildable today.

Consultation Call

BrockOtter records
30-60 minutes. Brock leads. Otter records automatically. Key: project type, funding type, DIR number, classifications, CPR platform, project requirements (Section 3, PLA, Skilled & Trained, OCIP), duration, employee count. Identify during the call if mid-project rescue applies.

Lead Type Decision

Brock
Two client types: Standard PW Contractor and Sub Oversight. Mid-project rescue is a condition that applies to either — not a separate type.

Standard PW Contractor

Brock / Anthy
Most common. Contractor performing public works. Needs CPR production, DAS forms, rate calculation, ECPR upload, CAC invoices. Mid-project rescue applies if behind on CPRs or received DIR notice.

Sub Oversight

Brock / Anthy
Prime contractor managing subs. Joint liability exposure. Call may include CEO, CFO, HR, accountants. Same close process, different estimate. Mid-project rescue applies if subs are non-compliant.

⚑ Mid-Project Rescue — Standard PW

Brock / Anthy
Contractor is already on a project and is behind, missed CPRs, or received DIR notice. Estimate includes restitution calculation ($35/employee/week) and catch-up CPR production. Higher urgency — faster close.

⚑ Mid-Project Rescue — Sub Oversight

Brock / Anthy
Subs are non-compliant. Prime at risk of joint liability. Estimate includes sub restitution review and audit risk assessment. Higher urgency — faster close.

Send Full Package

BrockClaude drafts
Brock sends same day as the call. Compliance estimate, price flyer, contract link, Standard Process SOP PDF. Claude drafts personalised cover email. Brock reviews and sends.

Contract Signed?

Zoho
Zoho monitors signature status. Retainer ($500, fully refundable) required alongside contract. Signed + retainer paid → Stage 3. No response → T+2, T+5, T+14 follow-up sequence.

T+2 — Gentle Nudge

ClaudeAuto-sends
Sent 2 days after the package. "Just checking in — did you get a chance to review everything? Happy to answer any questions." Claude drafts and sends automatically.

T+5 — Add Urgency

ClaudeAuto-sends
Sent 5 days after the package. Mentions the DAS 140 10-day window — if they recently signed a project contract, the clock is running. Makes the cost of delay concrete. Claude drafts and sends automatically.

T+14 — Brock Personal

BrockClaude drafts
Final touch. Brock personal outreach — short, warm, direct. Claude drafts, Brock reviews and sends personally. After this, no response → tagged cold in Zoho → Stage 6 stagnant recovery.

Tagged Cold in Zoho

ZohoClaude
No response after T+14. Claude tags the lead as cold in Zoho. Enters Stage 6 stagnant client recovery. Periodic light-touch re-engagement managed there.
