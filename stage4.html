Claude

ClaudeLead Consultant

Claude

CPR Signed Within 7 Days?

Claude
Claude monitors whether the CPR has been returned signed. If 7 days pass without a signature, a dashboard alert fires and a nudge is drafted. The 7-day threshold prevents signature stacking — the most common source of client frustration.

Signature Nudge

Claude draftsLead Consultant approves

PWCR Renewal Alert

ClaudeLead Consultant
45 days before PWCR expiration, Claude alerts the dashboard. Lead consultant notifies the client. The PWCR must be renewed annually for the contractor to legally perform public works in California. This is one of the most commonly missed deadlines — the alert makes it impossible to miss.

→ Stage 4: Active Project Loop

Trigger: first project starts
Stage 4 begins when the client's first project starts — not when onboarding completes. A client could be in Stage 3 (onboarded, no active project) for days or weeks before Stage 4 begins. That gap is managed through Stage 5 retention touchpoints until a project kicks off.
