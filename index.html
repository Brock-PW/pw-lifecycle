Standard PW Contractor

Client type
Contractor managing their own prevailing wage compliance. Needs help with: CPR production, DAS 140/142 forms, fringe benefit statements, ECPR uploads, and monthly CAC invoices. Most common client type. Full-service compliance partner relationship.

Sub Oversight Client

Client type
Prime contractor who wants compliance monitoring over their subcontractors. Key risk: prime contractors are jointly liable for sub noncompliance penalties. Services: Sub Oversight package — document collection, quality review, sub correspondence, compliance monitoring. See Sub Oversight price sheet for pricing. Subcontractor Start Up Fee: $200/sub.

DAS 140

AI draftsHuman sendsT+8 trigger
Alerts apprentice halls in project county. Due within 10 days of contract signing OR before first day on site, whichever comes first. All classifications need a DAS 140. Zoho fires reminder at T+8 if not yet sent.

Fringe benefit statement

Human producesClient signs
Documents where fringes are being paid (health & welfare, pension, vacation, training). Produced per classification/employee. Filed with first CPR. Must be updated whenever rates change. Annualization worthwhile on projects 4+ weeks.

Decision: Payroll received this week?

Zoho trigger
Yes → produce CPR. No → produce Statement of Non-Performance AND send client nudge at day 14. CPRs must be submitted within 30 days of work performed — track hard against this deadline.
