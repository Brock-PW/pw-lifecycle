The Differentiator: Full-Loop Submission

Brock — core pitchFrancis — built the tech
This is the single most important differentiator PW has relative to most compliance competitors. Most firms produce a CPR and hand it back to the contractor to upload. PW produces it AND submits it — directly into LCPTracker, eComply, Elations, or eMars. The contractor never logs in. Francis has built the technology that makes this seamless. This capability should appear in every channel, every pitch, and every piece of content.
