Target States — State PW Laws

Claude
NY, IL, WA, NJ, NV, OR all have robust state prevailing wage systems with requirements comparable to California. Same compliance pain points, same need for expert help. Key advantage: even fewer local compliance experts in these markets than in CA.

Federal Davis-Bacon

ClaudeBrock
Davis-Bacon Act applies to federally-funded construction projects nationwide. Key differences from CA state PW: no DAS forms, no CAC invoices, different CPR format (WH-347 vs CA format). PW already has a price sheet for Davis-Bacon. Strong angle for federal contractors in any state.

ICP Definition — National

Claude
Same core profile as CA: contractors on public works, no compliance partner. Filter by state, trade type, and whether the project is state-funded or federally-funded. AI segments B2B Rocket targeting lists accordingly.
