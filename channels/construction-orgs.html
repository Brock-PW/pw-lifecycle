Lead With Education, Not Sponsorship

Brock — strategy
Sponsorships and memberships create financial commitments without guaranteed access to contractors. The better play: offer to educate their members for free. Every org has members who need to understand prevailing wage compliance and very few experts willing to teach it without a sales agenda. PW fills that gap. The relationship and credibility that follows is worth more than a logo on a banner.

Target Org Profile

Claude
Any organisation where CA public works contractors gather. Trade associations, building councils, industry coalitions, regional contractor groups, specialty trade organisations, and conference organisers. The key criterion: their members include contractors performing or bidding on state or federally-funded public works projects in California.

Priority Org List

ClaudeMegan
AGC California is the anchor. Other strong targets: CALSCC (CA Subcontractors Association), CICPAC, ABC California, regional building industry associations. Specialty trade orgs with significant CA PW membership: NECA (electrical), SMACNA (sheet metal), MCA (mechanical), UA (plumbers/pipefitters), IBEW. Conference organisers in the CA construction space. Megan's network identifies warm entry points.

Format: Virtual First

BrockClaude — logistics
Virtual is the default format. Lower barrier for the org to say yes, wider geographic reach, easier to record, and Claude can handle all the logistics coordination without Brock needing to travel. Move to in-person trade shows selectively for events with the highest-quality CA public works contractor attendance.

Topic Menu — Brock Delivers

Brock
Four core topics Brock can deliver on request: "DAS 140 and 142 — What Every Contractor Must Know," "Surviving a DIR Audit," "Prevailing Wage Rate Changes — What's Coming and How to Prepare," "CPR Deadlines and Penalties." Each tailored to the org's member profile. More topics can be added as content library grows.

In-Person Trade Shows

Brock / Megan
Selective in-person presence at events with strong CA public works contractor attendance. The goal is always a speaking or education slot — not a booth. Face-to-face credibility compounds differently than virtual. Megan identifies the right events. Claude handles pre-event outreach to the organiser to secure the slot.

Session Logistics

ClaudeBrock — approves

Live Q&A → Lead Identification

ClaudeBrock
The Q&A is where the leads reveal themselves. Contractors asking specific questions about their current projects have active compliance problems — they are the highest-intent people in the room. Claude monitors the chat and question queue in real time, flags the most engaged participants to Brock. These get personalised follow-up, not just the standard attendee sequence.

Session Recording → Content

Claude
Every session is recorded. Claude clips the recording into: YouTube Shorts (key moments), a LinkedIn long-form post (key takeaways), Instagram Reels (compliance hooks from the session), and a blog post (full summary). One 45-minute session generates 30 days of content across all social channels.

Every Org Has a Member List

Claude
AGC, CALSCC, ABC, and every trade org maintains a registered member directory. These are pre-qualified, pre-segmented CA contractors who have self-identified as being in the construction industry. Often publicly accessible on the org website or available on request. This is one of the most valuable and underutilised lead sources available.

AI Directory Extraction

Claude
Claude identifies which directories are publicly accessible and extracts member information. Where directories require a request, Claude drafts the request. Cleans and segments by trade type, county, and company size. Loads into Zoho tagged with the org name as the lead source. This is a one-time task per org, then refresh annually.

Directory → B2B Rocket Targeting

ClaudeAutomated
Member lists feed directly into B2B Rocket outreach sequences as a separate, pre-qualified segment. These contractors have already self-identified as CA contractors — the targeting is more precise than a cold DIR scrape. Tag source as "[Org Name] member list" in Zoho for attribution tracking.

Directory → Social Lookalike Seed

ClaudeAll platforms
Member email lists uploaded to LinkedIn Matched Audiences, Meta Custom Audiences, and Google Customer Match. Each platform builds a lookalike of CA contractors matching the org member profile. Trade org member lookalikes are more targeted than the general DIR seed — they reflect specific trade types and company profiles.

Post-Event Attendee List

Zoho triggerClaude
Every webinar and trade show generates an attendee list. These are the highest-intent contractors in the entire channel — they showed up to a compliance session, which is the clearest possible signal of interest. Immediately loaded into Zoho, tagged with event name and org, and entered into the 14-day follow-up sequence.

List Compounding Effect

Claude
Each org engagement adds a new segmented, high-quality list. AGC members, CALSCC members, NECA members, event attendees — these lists accumulate over time and feed all outreach channels continuously. The more orgs PW engages with, the richer and more targeted the total lead database becomes. This is one of the highest-leverage long-term activities in Stage 1.
