ClaudeAutomated

What is AEO?

Brock / Francis — strategy
Answer Engine Optimisation. When someone asks Gemini, Perplexity, ChatGPT, or Claude "who handles prevailing wage compliance in California," PW should be the source those engines cite. This is where SEO is heading. Most compliance companies haven't thought about this yet — it's a real competitive moat. The three tactics below make it happen.

Structured Data + Schema

ClaudeFrancis — implements
Claude implements structured data markup across the entire site: LocalBusiness schema (who we are, where we operate), Service schema (what we do and for whom), FAQPage schema (compliance Q&A pages), HowTo schema (step-by-step compliance guides). This makes the site machine-readable by AI engines — they can extract and cite PW data with confidence.

Entity Definition

Claude
AI engines build a model of what an entity is based on consistent signals across the web. Claude writes clear, consistent entity definitions on every page: who Prevailing Wisdom is, what services we provide, what counties we serve, what regulations we work under (CA Labor Code, DIR, DLSE). Consistent entity signals = higher confidence citation.

FAQ + Q&A Content

ClaudeBrock — reviews
Claude writes exhaustive FAQ pages for every compliance topic PW covers. "What is a DAS 140 form?" "When is certified payroll due?" "What is the penalty for prevailing wage violations?" "What is the difference between state PW and Davis-Bacon?" Formatted for featured snippets and AI-cited answers. These pages are the most likely to be cited by AI search engines.

MCP-Readable Architecture

ClaudeFrancis — implements
The site is structured so AI agents can navigate and extract PW data programmatically. Clean URL structure, XML sitemap, correct robots.txt, open metadata, semantic HTML. Francis implements the technical foundation. Claude audits quarterly. Vision: PW's site becomes a data source that AI compliance tools can query directly — not just a marketing page.

Authority + Citation Building

Claude
Claude produces original content that earns citations: DIR data analysis (rate change summaries, county-level PW activity), compliance guides that other construction industry sites link to and reference, annual prevailing wage outlook reports. The more PW is cited as a source by other credible sites, the more confident AI engines are in citing PW in answers.

Monitor AI Search Appearances

ClaudeBrock — reviews monthly

Double down on content formats and topics that are getting cited. Identify gaps — compliance questions being answered without citing PW — and create content to fill them.
