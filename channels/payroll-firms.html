"We Handle Compliance. You Keep the Books."

Brock — core pitch
This is the central value proposition for every payroll firm conversation. They already have the client relationship and the payroll data. PW brings the compliance expertise their clients need but can't get from a payroll firm. Neither firm is stepping on the other's toes. Mutual clients get a more complete service. The firm looks better to their clients without doing any extra work.

Target Firm Profile

Claude
CA payroll, bookkeeping, and accounting firms that have construction or contractor clients. Small to mid-size firms are better targets than large national firms — they have more at stake in each client relationship and are more likely to actively refer. The ideal target already has public works contractor clients who are struggling with compliance and the firm has no good answer for them.

AI Firm Research

Claude
Claude identifies target firms through LinkedIn company searches, Google searches for CA payroll firms specialising in construction, Chamber of Commerce directories, and CA contractor association member lists (which often include their accounting and payroll partners). For each firm, Claude identifies the right contact — owner, managing partner, or payroll director.

Referral One-Pager

Claude
A clean, professional one-pager Claude produces for interested firms. Covers: what PW does, who we serve, how the referral works, what their clients get, and that it costs the firm nothing. Easy for the firm to share internally with colleagues or hand to a client when the compliance question comes up.

Partnership Discovery Call

Brock
30 minutes. Brock leads. Goal is to understand their client base — how many construction clients, how many do public works, what compliance questions come up, what gaps exist. Not a pitch call. Brock is listening and learning. The pitch comes naturally from understanding their situation.

Mutual Client Framing

Brock
The key reframe during the call. Their clients are already doing public works — they just don't have anyone handling the compliance side. PW fills that gap without touching the books, the payroll relationship, or anything the firm already does. The firm becomes a more complete solution provider without any extra work.

Referral Agreement Structure

Brock
Intentionally kept simple. The firm recommends PW to clients who need compliance help. PW delivers the service. No revenue share required unless the firm asks — the value exchange is that their clients get better served and they look good for making the introduction. Formal agreement optional but available if the firm wants it.

What the Firm Gets

Brock
Their clients get full-service coverage — payroll and compliance handled by specialists in each lane. The firm becomes a more complete service provider. They can introduce PW as a vetted partner, which builds trust with their clients. PW never touches their books, never competes for their work.

Paychex Integration — Live

Claude / FrancisLive
The Paychex integration is already live and working. For mutual clients, PW pulls payroll data directly from Paychex — no waiting for the client to send timesheets. Reduces client effort, speeds CPR production, reduces errors. This is the proof that data integration between a payroll platform and PW is possible and valuable.

What Integration Unlocks

Brock
Faster CPR turnaround. Fewer client touchpoints. Higher service quality for the mutual client. The integration becomes a tangible selling point for both firms — "your payroll data flows directly into compliance, seamlessly." It also increases switching costs for the mutual client, which benefits both firms.

Integration as Partnership Accelerator

Francis / Brock
The data integration conversation should only happen once a referral relationship is established and mutual client volume justifies the development work. Don't lead with it. The Paychex template means the second integration will be significantly faster than the first. Francis leads all technical integration scoping.

Future Integration Candidates

Francis / Brock
QuickBooks, Gusto, ADP are the priority candidates. Evaluate each by: mutual CA public works contractor client overlap, API availability and quality, development cost estimate, and projected volume. Only proceed where the ROI case is clear. Each successive integration should be faster using the accumulated template.

Integration Pitch Timing

Brock
Do not lead with integration in the initial outreach or discovery call. Establish the referral relationship first. Prove the mutual client value. The integration conversation happens naturally once both firms are seeing volume and the efficiency gain is obvious. Typically 6-12 months into an active partnership.
